Chicken Noodle Soup

I bet you're looking for a great homemade soup recipe right? Well, sorry. As I said, I can't cook.

To begin:

  1. A can of Chicken Noodle soup. Brand is irrelevant.
  2. A can opener. Extremely important.
  3. A bowl. Must be microwave safe.
  4. A microwave plugged into a source of electricity.
  • Place the can opener on the can, twisting in a circular motion until the can has been opened.
  • Empty the contents of the can into the microwave safe bowl.
  • Place the bowl into the microwave. Microwave on high for one to two minutes.
  • Remove soup from microwave. Let cool for two minutes before consumption.
