Delicious Thin Crust Pizza!

Okay, so now for something that actually qualifies as a recipe.

To start, you will need:

  • 170 grams of dough. Recipe can be found here.
  • An 8 inch diameter pizza pan of your choice.
  • Olive oil.
  • 6 tbps of a nice pizza sauce.
  • 120 grams of mozzarella cheese.
  • Various toppings of your choice.

Got all that? Then let's begin!

  1. Preheat oven to 250 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Roll out Pizza with a dough roller. Place rolled dough onto pan.
  3. Oil top of dough thoroughly. Flip dough and with hands, spread until it fits pan edge's closely.
  4. Flip dough over, and again spread dough until it fits pan edge's. Be careful not to rip the dough.
  5. Using fingers, create crust by 1 cm away from the edge into you tips the base of your finger.
  6. Add sauce to dough, then spread across the base of the dough by gentle shaking and rocking the pan.
  7. Add base 80 grams of cheese to top of sauce. Add any meat toppings on top of this base layer.
  8. Add any vegetable toppings onto top of the meat layer. Add 40 grams of cheese to complete toppings.
  9. Put pan in over, rotating 45 degrees every two minutes for ten minutes until crust has become golden brown. Check bottom constantly.
  10. Remove pan from oven, let cool five minutes. Cut into six or eight slices with pizza roller, and enjoy!
