Vaguely Edible Kraft Dinner Macaroni

Have you ever had a hungering for something you aren't entirely certain qualifies as food? Well, hunger no longer!

What you will need:

  • A box of Kraft Dinner Macaroni
  • A pot and roughly half a litre to one litre of water
  • An amount of milk that you will eye-ball instead of measuring
  • Roughly (gestures wildly) this much butter
  • No sense of taste.

Got all that, then let's begin!

  1. Fill your pot with an amount of water that you didn't bother measuring but is probably a bit more than half a litre.
  2. Set the pot to boil, which will take roughly 2 to 10 minutes depending on appliances.
  3. Open the Kraft Dinner package and pour the pasta into the water. Leave it to boil until soft.
  4. Strain pasta from water and dump the water. Add butter, cheese powder from package, and milk to pot, and mix over low heat.
  5. After sauce has reached desired texture of, "Looks good enough, I think," add pasta and mix until saturated.
  6. Pour into a bowl of you choice and eat until you remember how you really don't like this stuff. Throw out mostly uneaten remains.

And there you go, that's how to make a bowl of delicious probably edible Kraft Dinner Macaroni!
