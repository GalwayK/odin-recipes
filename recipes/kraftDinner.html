Vaguely Edible Kraft Dinner Macaroni

Have you ever had a hungering for something you aren't entirely certain qualifies as food? Well, hunger no longer!

What you will need:

  1. A box of Kraft Dinner Macaroni
  2. A pot and roughly half a litre to one litre of water
  3. An amount of milk that you will eye-ball instead of measuring
  4. Roughly (gestures wildly) this much butter
  5. No sense of taste.

Got all that, then let's begin!

  • Fill your pot with an amount of water that you didn't bother measuring but is probably a bit more than half a litre.
  • Set the pot to boil, which will take roughly 2 to 10 minutes depending on appliances.
  • Open the Kraft Dinner package and pour the pasta into the water. Leave it to boil until soft.
  • Strain pasta from water and dump the water. Add butter, cheese powder from package, and milk to pot, and mix over low heat.
  • After sauce has reached desired texture of, "Looks good enough, I think," add pasta and mix until saturated.
  • Pour into a bowl of you choice and eat until you remember how you really don't like this stuff. Throw out mostly uneaten remains.

And there you go, that's how to make a bowl of delicious probably edible Kraft Dinner Macaroni!
